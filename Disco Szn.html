About Rhythm Resilience

“Rhythm Resilience” brings back the magic of the legendary Studio 54. Combining dance with immersive virtual reality, users can step into a remastered 1977 New York City environment. The VR experience allows dancers to practice, perform, and engage with cutting-edge technology, improving their moves through Azure Machine Learning analysis.

Our Project

Our virtual reality platform allows users to engage in real-time dance experiences, powered by Azure Machine Learning for performance feedback. Whether you’re a beginner or an expert dancer, we have the tools to help you thrive.
